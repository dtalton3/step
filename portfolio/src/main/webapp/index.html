"Hay, my name ih Dehmun Taltun"

Since I've been in college at Georgia Tech, I've been teased for my dialect.
I am from Macon, Georgia (which is a city, but I guess its the country in the middle of nowhere) and that's just how we talk down here.
From the slang and "lingo" I employ to the southern drawl I deliver it with, the way I talk is just something that makes me... me. In my opinion, the way I talk is the best way to go about communicating, so you can tease me about it. I am not insecure of it.

Something I was insecure about, though, was my weight

For most of my life I was pretty obese. I had always been a big guys and say it as an inevitability, but I never liked it. I felt like my size was the first impression that characterized who I was before I got the opportunity to do so.

... That's not how it actually works
... but thats how I saw it at the time.

In 2019, I finally decided to take control of my situation. I changed my diet and started to be a lot more active.

Honestly, I feel its my biggest accomplishment to date. I am not satisfied yet, though. I feel I could still lose some more weight, but I definitely have a healthier view of my self-image.
While, in hindsight, it doesn't make sense (most insecurities never do, to be honest), this really opened the door to make me feel like I can be more of myself.

So... who am I, really?

My name is Desmond Talton. I am a rising 3rd year Computer Science major at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I am particularly interested in web app development on the front and back end. Overall, I am just a guy who enjoys having fun and being creative.
